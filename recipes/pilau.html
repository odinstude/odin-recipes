How To Cook The Best Pilau

Recipe Description

Pilau Pro is a meat-loving dish that is made with the finest ingredients in the world.

Ingredients

Steps Pilau

  1. Wash the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Soak the rice in water for about 30 minutes to soften.
  3. Chop onions, garlic, and ginger finely.
  4. Heat oil in a cooking pot over medium heat.
  5. Add whole spices like cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves to the hot oil and sauté until fragrant.
  6. Add chopped onions, garlic, and ginger to the pot and sauté until golden brown.
  7. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  8. Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pot, stirring to coat the rice with the onion-tomato mixture.
  9. Add water or broth to the pot, along with salt, turmeric, cumin, and other spices according to taste.
  10. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and simmer until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  11. Once cooked, fluff the pilau with a fork and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
  12. Garnish with fresh cilantro or mint leaves before serving, if desired.
  13. Serve the pilau hot with your favorite accompaniments and enjoy!
